OUR STORY

Founded over 25 years ago, MK&R Group began as a small family business with a simple vision: to deliver construction projects that exceed expectations while building lasting relationships with our clients.

From humble beginnings with a single mobile crane, we've grown into Queensland's premier construction and crane solutions provider. Our journey has been marked by continuous innovation, strategic growth, and an unwavering commitment to safety and quality.

Today, we're proud to have completed over 500 projects across Queensland and Australia, each one a testament to our dedication to excellence and our ability to turn complex challenges into successful outcomes.
